How to use barcode scanning production

Installing the barcode scanning app on Odoo

  1. Log in to your Odoo account.
  2. Click on the application icon in the top left corner of the screen.
  3. Click on "Apps" from the appearing menu.
  4. Search for " Barcode mrp production" in the search field and click on it.
  5. Click on the "Install" button to install the app.
  6. Once installed, you can use the barcode scanning app in Odoo.

Using the barcode scanning app in Odoo

  1. Log in to your Odoo account.
  2. Select the "Barcode Scanning" app from the application menu.
  3. Point the device's camera at the barcode on the production.
  4. Once the barcode is scanned, Odoo will display the corresponding production information on the screen.
